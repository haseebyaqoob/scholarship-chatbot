USAID Merit & Need Scholarship
Level: Undergraduate and Postgraduate
Fields: Engineering, Medicine, Social Sciences
Domicile: Any province (Pakistan-wide)
Country: Pakistan
Funding Type: Merit-based
CGPA Requirement: Minimum 3.2
Amount: Up to USD 5,000/year (approx. PKR 1,400,000) covering tuition, living expenses, and books

Eligibility:
- Pakistani citizen
- CGPA >= 3.2
- Enrolled in or applying to engineering, medicine, or social sciences
- Two tracks available:
    Merit Track: Academic achievement only
    Need Track: Merit + demonstrated financial need

Benefits:
- Up to $5,000/year covering tuition, living, and books
- US alumni mentorship and professional network access
- Both undergraduate and postgraduate students eligible

Required Documents:
- 2 faculty recommendation letters
- Personal statement (500 words)
- Official transcripts
- CNIC copy

Deadline: April 10
Status: Active
Contact: usaid.scholarships@pk.usaid.gov
