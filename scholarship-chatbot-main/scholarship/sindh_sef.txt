Sindh Endowment Fund (SEF) Scholarship 2025

The Sindh Endowment Fund Scholarship is a need-based postgraduate scholarship for students from Sindh province. It is administered by the Sindh Education Department and aims to support financially constrained students in pursuing higher education.

Eligibility:
- Sindh domicile certificate is mandatory
- Postgraduate level students (Masters or equivalent)
- Minimum CGPA: 3.0 out of 4.0
- Must demonstrate genuine financial need
- Must be enrolled in a recognized university in Pakistan
- Sindhi students studying internationally may also be eligible (check latest guidelines)

Amount:
Up to PKR 250,000 per academic year.
The amount is disbursed in installments based on academic performance and fund availability.

Deadline:
1 December 2025

Application Process:
- Apply via the official Sindh Education Portal (education.sindh.gov.pk)
- Submit a valid Sindh domicile certificate
- Provide certified academic transcripts
- Financial need documentation: income certificate and affidavit
- University enrollment certificate required
- Applications submitted after the deadline will not be considered

Status:
Active - Applications are open for the 2025 academic year.
