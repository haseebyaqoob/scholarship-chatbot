Punjab Educational Endowment Fund (PEEF)
Level: Undergraduate
Fields: Any discipline
Domicile: Punjab (mandatory)
Country: Pakistan
Funding Type: Need-based
CGPA Requirement: Minimum 2.8
Amount: Tuition up to PKR 80,000/year + hostel charges + one-time laptop grant

Eligibility:
- Punjab domicile certificate is mandatory
- Enrolled in an undergraduate programme
- CGPA >= 2.8
- Family income < PKR 120,000/month
- Matric marks >= 60%
- Must not be receiving any other financial aid

Priority Groups:
- Students from rural areas
- Orphans and widows' children
- Children of grade 1-4 government employees
- Students with disabilities

Benefits:
- Tuition fee up to PKR 80,000/year
- Hostel charges covered
- One-time laptop grant for eligible students

Application Process:
1. Register at peef.org.pk
2. Attach Punjab domicile certificate
3. Submit family income proof
4. Attach Matric/FSc result copies

Deadline: March 20
Status: Active
Contact: info@peef.org.pk
