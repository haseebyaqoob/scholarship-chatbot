Aga Khan Foundation International Scholarship Programme
Level: Postgraduate (Masters / PhD only — undergraduates not eligible)
Fields: Health & nutrition, Education, Rural development, Civil society
Domicile: Any province (Pakistan-wide)
Country: Pakistan (also open to India, Kenya, Tanzania, Tajikistan)
Funding Type: Need-based (50% grant + 50% interest-free loan)
CGPA Requirement: Minimum 3.0
Amount: Full tuition + living stipend + return airfare + health insurance

Eligibility:
- Pakistani national (or citizen of eligible AKF countries)
- Postgraduate level only — Masters or PhD
- CGPA >= 3.0
- Priority fields: health & nutrition, education, rural development, civil society
- Must demonstrate genuine financial need
- Must commit to returning home post-study (3-year bond)

Financial Model:
- 50% of award is an outright grant
- 50% is an interest-free loan repaid after gaining employment
- This model ensures sustainability of the programme

Benefits:
- Full tuition coverage
- Monthly living stipend
- Return airfare (economy class)
- Health insurance for study duration

Application Steps:
1. Download application form from AKF national office
2. Submit certified transcripts from every university attended
3. Provide two academic reference letters
4. Write a personal statement (500–800 words)
5. Proof of university admission or pending applications
6. Family financial documentation (tax returns, salary slips)
7. Attend interview if shortlisted

Timeline:
- Applications open: January 15
- Deadline: April 15
- Interviews: May – June
- Results announced: July
- Programme starts: September

Bond: Recipient must return to home country and contribute professionally for at least 3 years.
Status: Active
