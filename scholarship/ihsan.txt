Ihsan Trust Scholarship
Level: Undergraduate
Fields: Any discipline
Domicile: Any province (Pakistan-wide)
Country: Pakistan
Funding Type: Need-based
CGPA Requirement: Minimum 2.0
Amount: PKR 3,000/month stipend + medical assistance + career counseling

Eligibility:
- Any Pakistani university student (undergraduate)
- CGPA >= 2.0 (lowest barrier of all listed scholarships)
- No income cap — priority given to most vulnerable cases

Priority Groups:
- Orphans
- Widows' children
- Students with disabilities
- Extremely low-income families
- Students at risk of dropping out due to financial hardship

Benefits:
- PKR 3,000/month living stipend
- Medical assistance support
- Career counseling and mentorship
- Connects students with 50+ partner universities

Goal:
To retain at-risk students in higher education who would otherwise be forced to drop out.
