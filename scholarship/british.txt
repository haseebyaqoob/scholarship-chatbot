British Council STEM Scholarship for Women — Pakistan
Level: Postgraduate (Masters only)
Fields: CS, Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, Data Science, Biology, Chemistry
Domicile: Any province (Pakistan-wide)
Country: UK (study destination)
Funding Type: Merit-based
CGPA Requirement: Minimum 3.6
Amount: Full tuition up to GBP 30,000 + GBP 1,000–1,200/month living + return airfare + visa fees

Target Group:
Women in STEM disciplines seeking Masters-level study in the United Kingdom.

Eligibility:
- Female applicants only
- Enrolled in or applying to a STEM Masters programme in the UK
- CGPA >= 3.6
- English: IELTS 6.5 with no band below 6.0, or TOEFL iBT 90+
- Must NOT already be in the UK
- UK nationals and permanent UK residents are not eligible

Fields Covered:
Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, Data Science, Biology, Chemistry

Partner Universities:
University of Edinburgh, University of Manchester, Imperial College London,
University of Birmingham, University of Leeds, King's College London, Newcastle University

Full Award Covers:
- Tuition fees up to GBP 30,000
- GBP 1,000–1,200/month living allowance
- Return economy airfare
- GBP 500 study materials allowance
- GBP 250 warm clothing allowance
- UK visa fees

Application Documents:
1. Personal statement (600 words) — course fit, career goals, and reasons for choosing the UK
2. Two academic or professional references
3. Certified university transcripts
4. IELTS/TOEFL certificate (must be under 2 years old)
5. CNIC or passport copy

Interview Process:
30-minute video call covering academic preparation and commitment to return to Pakistan.

Bond: Recipient must return and work professionally in Pakistan for 5 years post-study.
Deadline: July 1
Status: Active
