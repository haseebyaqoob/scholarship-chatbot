HEC Need-Based Scholarship
Level: Undergraduate (BS / BE / B.Tech)
Fields: Any discipline
Domicile: Any province (Pakistan-wide)
Country: Pakistan
Funding Type: Need-based
CGPA Requirement: Minimum 2.5
Amount: Full tuition + PKR 5,000/month stipend + PKR 10,000/year book allowance

Eligibility:
- Pakistani national enrolled in a recognised HEC university
- BS/BE/B.Tech student (undergraduate only)
- CGPA >= 2.5
- Family income < PKR 500,000/year
- Must not be receiving any other scholarship

Benefits:
- Full tuition fee coverage
- PKR 5,000/month living stipend
- PKR 10,000/year book allowance

Application Process:
1. Apply via the HEC online portal (hec.gov.pk)
2. Upload income certificate (family income < PKR 500,000)
3. Upload university enrollment letter
4. Upload domicile certificate
5. Submit before the deadline

Renewal: Reviewed every semester based on academic performance.
Deadline: March 31
Status: Active
Contact: scholarships@hec.gov.pk
