ITU MS AI Excellence Scholarship 2025

The Information Technology University (ITU) MS Artificial Intelligence Excellence Scholarship is a merit-based award for high-achieving students admitted to the MS Artificial Intelligence program at ITU Lahore. It aims to attract top talent and advance AI research in Pakistan.

Eligibility:
- Must have secured admission in the MS Artificial Intelligence program at ITU Lahore
- Postgraduate level (MS only)
- Minimum CGPA: 3.3 out of 4.0 from a recognized institution
- Merit-based award; financial need is not a criterion
- Strong background in computer science, mathematics, or a related field preferred
- Fresh graduates and working professionals are both eligible

Amount:
PKR 400,000 per academic year.
The scholarship covers tuition and may include a monthly stipend component depending on availability.

Deadline:
20 November 2025

Application Process:
- Apply simultaneously with the MS AI admissions process at ITU
- Submit academic transcripts from all previous institutions
- An interview may be required as part of the merit evaluation
- Statement of purpose is required highlighting research interests
- Two reference letters from academic supervisors preferred

Status:
Active - Applications for the 2025-2026 academic year are open.
