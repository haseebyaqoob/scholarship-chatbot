Fulbright Scholarship — Pakistan
Level: Postgraduate (Masters 2 yr / PhD up to 5 yr) + FLTA + Hubert H. Humphrey Fellowship
Fields: Any discipline
Domicile: Any province (Pakistan-wide)
Country: USA (study destination)
Funding Type: Merit-based
CGPA Requirement: Minimum 3.7
Amount: Full tuition + USD 1,500–2,200/month stipend + round-trip airfare + medical insurance

About Fulbright:
The most prestigious US government scholarship, administered in Pakistan by USEFP (United States Educational Foundation Pakistan).

Programme Types:
- Fulbright Masters (2 years in the USA)
- Fulbright PhD (up to 5 years in the USA)
- FLTA — Foreign Language Teaching Assistant (9-month Urdu teaching placement)
- Hubert H. Humphrey (HHH) Fellowship for mid-career professionals

Ineligibility:
- Dual nationals
- US green card holders
- Applicants already residing in the USA

Benefits:
- Full tuition at a US university placed by IIE
- USD 1,500–2,200/month living stipend
- Round-trip economy airfare
- Full medical insurance
- Settling-in allowance
- Pre-departure orientation in Islamabad
- Gateway orientation in Washington DC

Selection Phases:
Phase 1 — Online application: essays, transcripts, 3 reference letters
Phase 2 — Written test: academic writing and critical reasoning
Phase 3 — Panel interview: leadership, community impact, goals
Phase 4 — University placement by IIE (Institute of International Education)
Phase 5 — J-1 visa processing and pre-departure orientation

Selection Criteria (ranked):
1. Academic excellence
2. Leadership potential
3. Contribution to Pakistan
4. Clarity of goals
5. English proficiency

Post-Award Obligation:
Recipient must return to Pakistan and work for at least 2 years after completing the programme.

Deadline: June 30
