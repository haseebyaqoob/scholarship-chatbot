DAAD — German Academic Exchange Service Scholarship
Level: Postgraduate (Masters / PhD / Postdoc)
Fields: Engineering, Agriculture, Economics, Public Health
Domicile: Any province (Pakistan-wide)
Country: Germany (study destination)
Funding Type: Merit-based
CGPA Requirement: Minimum 3.5
Amount: EUR 861/month (Masters), EUR 1,200/month (PhD), EUR 1,750/month (Postdoc)

About DAAD:
DAAD is the world's largest international academic exchange funder.
Key programme for Pakistani applicants: EPOS (development-related postgraduate programmes).

Eligibility:
- Pakistani applicant applying for Masters or PhD study in Germany
- CGPA >= 3.5
- 2+ years of professional experience preferred
- Age limit: 36 years at time of application
- English B2 or German B2 proficiency depending on chosen programme

Benefits:
- Monthly stipend: EUR 861 (Masters) / EUR 1,200 (PhD) / EUR 1,750 (Postdoc)
- Full health insurance coverage
- Travel allowance (return flights)
- EUR 460/year study & research allowance
- Subsidised German language course before programme start

Required Documents:
1. DAAD online application form (portal.daad.de)
2. CV in Europass format
3. Motivation letter (max 2 pages)
4. Two professor recommendation letters
5. Certified transcripts (translated if not in English or German)
6. Language certificate: IELTS/TOEFL or Goethe/TestDaF
7. Sample academic work (published paper or thesis chapter)

Acceptance Rate: 5–10% (highly competitive)
Deadline: May 1
Status: Active
Contact: DAAD IC Islamabad — House 4, Street 12, F-8/3 | daad-ic@isb.paknet.com.pk
