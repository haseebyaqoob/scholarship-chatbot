Punjab Merit Scholarship 2023

The Punjab Merit Scholarship was a government-funded scholarship program for high-achieving undergraduate students domiciled in Punjab, Pakistan. It rewarded academic excellence and was not based on financial need.

Eligibility:
- Punjab domicile certificate required
- Undergraduate students enrolled in recognized universities
- Minimum CGPA: 3.2 out of 4.0
- Merit-based award only; financial need was not considered
- Students must have been enrolled at the time of application

Amount:
PKR 150,000 per year.

Deadline:
30 September 2023

Status:
EXPIRED - This scholarship cycle closed on 30 September 2023. The 2023 cycle has ended and no new applications are being accepted. Students should check the Punjab Higher Education Department website for announcements regarding future scholarship cycles.

Application Process (for reference):
- Applications were submitted through the Punjab Higher Education portal
- Domicile certificate and academic transcripts were required
- Merit list was published by the department
